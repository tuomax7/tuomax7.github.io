Project Title: Designing a Construction Block

Created by: Tuomas Nummela

Date: February 11, 2025

Introduction

This assignment explores the concept of a construction/building block through the design of a new material or block. Using LEGO as an example, which is known for its versatility in tinkering and creative applications, the task is to come up with an innovative building block idea.

Design Concept

The goal of this project is to create a new building block that can address a specific technical challenge. For instance, designing a block that can interlock in multiple ways or one that adapts to different environments (e.g., building in tight spaces, creating lightweight structures).

Technical Hurdle Tackled

The specific challenge that this design addresses is the difficulty of constructing stable structures in constrained or irregular spaces. The block I propose allows for easy interlocking at different angles, enabling flexibility in design while maintaining structural integrity in non-standard configurations.

Conclusion

This process of designing a new building block was challenging but rewarding. The ability to address a specific technical problem, while also exploring new possibilities for construction materials, allowed for a great deal of creativity and innovation. The progression of sketches shows how the block evolved to meet the functional requirements while maintaining usability.
