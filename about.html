My Background

Hello! I am a fourth-year Computer Science student from Aalto University in Finland, passionate about frontend web development and Human-Computer Interaction. Currently, I am on a study exchange at the University of Twente, specializing in Interaction Technology.

Alongside my studies, I work as a Full Stack Developer in a software company where I contribute to web and mobile client projects using mainly React and Node.js. Previously, I have also worked as a Teaching Assistant in programming at Aalto University.

Interests

I enjoy building intuitive and interesting user interfaces, focusing on usability and modern development practices. I am especially interested in design systems and utilizing generative AI in user interface design as a form of tinkering.
